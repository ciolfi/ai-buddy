Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 1, 2025  ·  Last updated: January 1, 2025

🔒

The short version: we collect nothing.

NEURON runs entirely inside your browser. No data — not your prompts, your documents, your conversations, or your device information — is ever transmitted to any server. There is no backend. There is no database. There is no analytics.

01 About NEURON

NEURON is a Progressive Web Application (PWA) that provides AI-powered chat and document analysis entirely within your web browser. All language model inference is performed locally on your device using WebGPU and WebAssembly technology. NEURON does not operate any servers that process user content.

02 Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, transmit, or process any of the following:

  • Your chat messages, prompts, or conversation history
  • Documents or files you upload for RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)
  • Your name, email address, or any identifying information
  • Your IP address or approximate location
  • Device identifiers, browser fingerprints, or hardware information
  • Usage patterns, session data, or behavioral analytics
  • Cookies or tracking pixels of any kind

03 How NEURON Stores Data Locally

NEURON uses your browser's built-in storage mechanisms solely to function on your device:

  • IndexedDB — stores downloaded AI model weight files so they do not need to be re-downloaded on every visit. This data never leaves your device.
  • Cache API — stores the application shell (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, fonts) for offline use via the service worker. This data never leaves your device.

All locally stored data can be deleted at any time using the Clear App Data button inside the application, or through your browser's built-in storage management settings.

04 Third-Party Services

NEURON does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or data-processing services.

AI model weights are downloaded from MLC AI's public CDN (huggingface.co) on first use. This download is a standard HTTPS file transfer. The CDN provider may log your IP address as part of normal server operations, subject to their own privacy policy. NEURON has no control over or access to these logs.

If you access NEURON through a hosting provider such as Vercel, that provider's standard access logs (IP address, timestamp, requested URL) may be retained per their own privacy policy.

05 Children's Privacy

NEURON does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Because no personal data is collected, NEURON is structurally compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

06 Your Rights

Because NEURON does not collect or store any personal data on external servers, there is no personal data held by us to access, correct, export, or delete. All data stored locally on your device is fully under your control at all times.

Residents of the European Economic Area (EEA), California, and other jurisdictions with data privacy rights retain those rights. If you have any questions about how your data is handled, please contact us using the details below.

07 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of NEURON after any changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
